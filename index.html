Powering the Future

Nuclear energy is one of the most efficient and sustainable sources of electricity. Explore how it works, its benefits, and its role in building a cleaner world.

Quick Facts

  • Nuclear power provides about 10% of the world’s electricity.
  • It produces near-zero greenhouse gas emissions during operation.
  • One uranium fuel pellet can generate as much energy as 1 ton of coal.

Why Nuclear Energy Matters

As the world faces climate change, nuclear energy offers a reliable solution to reduce carbon emissions while meeting growing energy demands. It complements renewable sources like solar and wind by providing consistent power, day and night.
